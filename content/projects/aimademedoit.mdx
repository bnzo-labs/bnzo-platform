AIMadeMeDoIt started from a simple premise: what if every app took its absurd premise completely seriously?

The internet is full of joke apps that wink at you. What we wanted was the opposite — beautifully designed, polished micro-experiences where the joke is the premise, not the execution. An Overthinking Detector built with the same care as a real productivity tool. An Apology Quality Scorer with a real rubric and real scoring logic.

The technical challenge is scale. 21 apps with consistent quality means you can't build them one at a time. The monorepo structure lets agents work in parallel — Wave 2 ships 7 apps simultaneously, Wave 3 ships 7 more. The shared package handles the plumbing: Claude API calls, local storage, social sharing, and the watermark that ties the brand together.

CSS is custom per app, not Tailwind. Every app needs to look intentional, not templated. That constraint adds time but it's non-negotiable.

Launch strategy mirrors the product: one app per day for 21 days on Twitter/X, then a Product Hunt bundle launch.
